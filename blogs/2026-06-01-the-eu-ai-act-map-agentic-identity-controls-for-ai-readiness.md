AI governance is quickly becoming a business requirement as AI agents gain the ability to access company systems, handle sensitive data, use APIs, automate tasks, and make decisions for users. This post maps how the EU AI Act's obligations for high-risk AI systems translate into specific agentic identity controls that organizations should implement today to achieve compliance readiness.
