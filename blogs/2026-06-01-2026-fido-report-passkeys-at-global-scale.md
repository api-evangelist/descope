World Password Day lands on the first Thursday of May, and its intent remains the same as when it debuted in 2013: raise awareness for digital hygiene and encourage everyone to audit their password security. This post reviews the 2026 FIDO Alliance report on passkey adoption, showing how the industry has moved from experimentation to global deployment across major consumer platforms.
