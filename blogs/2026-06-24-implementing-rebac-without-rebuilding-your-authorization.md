The article explains how organizations can migrate from Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to Relationship-Based Access Control (ReBAC) incrementally using Descope, moving away from brittle role hierarchies toward a model where access follows ownership, membership, and delegation through explicitly defined relationships between users and resources.
